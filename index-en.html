What we do

Services

01 —

Data Analysis

We source, clean, model, and interpret large volumes of public and institutional data to surface precise patterns at the most granular level possible. You bring the dataset; we deliver an actionable diagnostic with findings ranked by impact.

02 —

Maps, Dashboards & Infographics

We translate complex evidence into maps, dashboards, and infographics that any decision-maker can read in under 30 seconds. No visual noise, no decoration — only what you need to act.

03 —

Public Policy Advisory

We design, diagnose, and evaluate evidence-based policies. Territorial context, institutional history, political feasibility — the human judgment no model can replicate. That is exactly what we bring to the table.

04 —

Training & Workshops

For government teams and organizations that want to make better decisions with their own data. We don't teach tools — we build analytical capacity that generates lasting institutional knowledge.
